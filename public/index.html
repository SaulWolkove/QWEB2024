Queen's Black Computer Science Society
We are the organization that provides supportive space for black students pursuing their passion for technology.
Our Goals
NETWORKING
Connect with peers and industry professionals, bridging the gap between academia and industry. Our events and sessions aim to build a supportive network for black students in computer science, fostering diversity and innovation.
PROFESSIONAL
DEVELOPMENT
Sharpen your skills with interactive workshops and hands-on sessions. We're here to support your growth, providing resources, mentorship, and insights to help you thrive in the ever-evolving tech world.
COMMUNITY
Join a safe and inclusive community for black students passionate about computer science. We celebrate achievements, foster collaboration, and create a sense of belonging beyond the screen.
